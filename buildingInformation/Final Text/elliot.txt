About
The Elliot Hall of Music was named after Purdue president Edward C. Elliot, who served as president of Purdue from 1922 to 1945. The stage of the Elliot Hall of Music is one of the largest in the country, comparable in size to the Dolby Theatre stage in Hollywood. With a seating capacity of 6,005, Elliot Hall is one of the largest proscenium theaters in the world.
